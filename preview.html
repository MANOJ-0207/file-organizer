Date-Time Format Instructions ⓘ ▼
  • hh – Hour (2-digit, 24-hour)
  • mm – Minute
  • ss – Second
  • yy – Year (2-digit)
  • yyyy – Year (4-digit)
  • dd – Day
  • MM – Month
  • mon – Short Month
  • month – Full Month
  • day – Short Day
  • DAY – Full Day
  • Include these formats as needed in prefix/suffix date format separated by special characters. (Not Alphabets and Numbers)
